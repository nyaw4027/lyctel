Privacy Policy

This policy outlines how Lynctel collects, manages, uses, and safeguards your transactional and personal information across our platforms.

1. Information We Collect

To offer a streamlined, responsive marketplace experience, Lynctel processes distinct details when you sign up, build storefront spaces, or finalize transactional flows:

  • Identity Profiles: Legal name, brand/merchant tags, and business identifiers.
  • Contact Routes: Valid email profiles, active phone numbers, and physical fulfillment/billing destinations.
  • System Telemetry: Log points, standard IP parameters, browser variants, and processing identifiers recorded natively via secure session cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

The acquired datasets serve targeted operational functions to ensure optimal platform maintenance:

Fulfillment & Operations

Processing live orders, initializing custom merchant dashboard workflows, and enabling storefront layouts.

Communication Management

Issuing order alerts, safety verifications, system adjustments, and platform resolution assistance.

3. Security Infrastructure

Data storage pipelines execute inside restricted, authenticated environments. We utilize enterprise-grade Secure Socket Layer (SSL/TLS) encryptions across transmissions to mitigate unauthorized breaches, keeping local records siloed from direct public access layers.

4. Third-Party Services

Lynctel explicitly isolates data loops from third-party advertising clusters. Information sharing occurs solely with specialized partners executing direct logistical tasks—such as integrated financial clearers, processing gateways, and domestic courier delivery networks. These external nodes handle your information under strict data processing agreements.

5. Your Controls & Rights

You retain total sovereignty over your personal data footprint on Lynctel:

Modification & Review: Direct profile values can be reviewed and amended inside your user settings anytime.

Erasure Rights: You can request permanent database purges of active personal information records by filing a request with support.

6. Have Questions?

For technical transparency details, general security inquiries, or requests regarding data erasure, get in touch with our security compliance team.
